General Information

What is Android Network Traces?

Many smartphone apps collect and send a great deal of data to third-party websites. The goal of this site is to help people better understand Who knows What about us and Why. By Who, we mean these third-party websites. By What, we mean what data they are collecting about us. By Why, we mean the purpose of this data collection, e.g. for advertising, for maps, for backups, and so on.
