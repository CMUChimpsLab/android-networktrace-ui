Who knows What about us
and Why?

Many smartphone apps gather and send lots of sensitive data to various third parties around the Internet.
However, it’s hard to know exactly what apps are sending out what data and why.

Collecting Data

As a team of researchers at Carnegie Mellon University, we collected network traces from thousands of apps, and analyzed them into categories of data types.
In this website we present data visualizations showing ‘who’ collects our data, ‘what’ data they collect, 'where' do they send our data to, and 'why' the data is being sent.

Where does my data go?

In this data visualization, we want to show the users what sort of network activity is being carried out by their apps. We present queryable data visualizations of 'where' and 'why' data is being sent.
